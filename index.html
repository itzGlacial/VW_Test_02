What is Vaporwave
Vaporwave is a subgenre of eletronic music which features
heavy sampling and editing, surreal and futuristic themes, and 80's
90's imagery, musical styles and consumer culture.

Blank Banshee and Vaporwave's Rebirth
In September of 2012, Patrick Driscoll, A.K.A Blank Banshee would
rise to become the savior of Vaporwave releasing his S/T album, Blank
Banshee Zero.
Blank Banshee Zero would reform the Vaporwave genre by introducing
a classic Vaporwave sound fused with a trap beat and heavy drop bass.

A Constant Evolution
Vaporwave is a rare succes among internet subcultures and rightly so.
Where most attempts at something similar failed, pioneers in the Vaporwave
movement have managed to steer the development of its music, aesthetic and
culture.

Vaporwave now is more than a "mere" subculture. It's the embodiment of
expression for a unique group of people, telling their story of existence in a
diverse and interesting number of ways.
